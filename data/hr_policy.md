# Darwinbox Mock HR Policy Handbook

## Leave Policy

Full-time employees receive 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave is accrued monthly and unused annual leave can be carried forward up to 10 days into the next year. Requests for 1 or 2 working days should be submitted at least 2 working days before the start date. Requests for 3 or more working days should be submitted at least 7 working days before the start date.

Employees also receive 10 days of sick leave each calendar year. Sick leave can be applied retrospectively within 3 working days when supported by a manager note or medical certificate. Sick leave cannot be encashed or carried forward.

Maternity leave is 26 weeks for the first two surviving children and 12 weeks for subsequent children, in line with Indian statutory norms. The employee should notify HR at least 8 weeks before the expected start date when practical. Paternity leave is 10 working days and should be used within 6 months of the child's birth.

Leave requests are routed to the reporting manager first. If the request is longer than 10 working days, HR operations also reviews staffing impact before final approval.

## Payroll And Payslips

Payroll is processed on the last working day of each month. Reimbursement claims approved by the 20th of the month are included in the same month's payroll; later approvals move to the next payroll cycle.

Payslips are available in the employee portal after payroll is finalized. Employees can request a payslip for any completed month in the current or previous financial year. Payroll corrections must be raised within 15 calendar days of payslip publication.

## Onboarding

New hires must complete identity verification, bank details, tax declarations, device acknowledgement, and policy acceptance before their joining date. The onboarding buddy is assigned by the hiring manager and introduced during the first week.

Probation lasts 90 days unless a role-specific employment letter states otherwise. Managers should complete check-ins at day 30, day 60, and day 90.
